FAQ

Frequently asked questions

What kind of websites do you build? We build everything from portfolio sites, landing pages, and company profiles to full-featured web applications, e-commerce platforms, and more — tailored to your goals.
Will you revise the design if I’m not satisfied? Yes, we offer revision rounds to ensure the design and functionality meet your expectations. We value collaboration and clear communication throughout the process.
How long does a project usually take? Project timelines vary based on complexity. A simple landing page can be completed in a few days, while full web apps may take a few weeks. We always aim to deliver efficiently with quality.
Do you offer maintenance or support after launch? Yes! We offer post-launch support options, including bug fixes, updates, and optional long-term maintenance packages to keep your site running smoothly.
Can you work with existing designs or sites? Absolutely! We can improve, modernize, or rebuild existing websites. Whether it’s fixing issues or redesigning from the ground up, we’re ready to help.
What technologies or platforms do you use? We work with modern technologies such as HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, PHP (Laravel, Cake PHP), MySQL, and Vue js. We also integrate third-party APIs and tools based on your project needs.
